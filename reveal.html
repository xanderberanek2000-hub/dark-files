About
Who is not_darkarrow?

I've been streaming on Twitch as "Darkarrow" for 2+ years and wanted to help grow my audience by creating a public 24/7 Minecraft server. I spent months learning and working on building a Minecraft server inspired by some of my favorite servers and minigames.

After making a Minecraft server people can play when I'm not live, someone recommended I use Tebex to handle transactions and make the money back I spent working on this server. I am fully open to any suggestions or help for this server, and I try to keep it fair for players that don't spend money.
